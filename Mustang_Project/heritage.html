The Mustang Legacy

Since 1964, the Ford Mustang has defined American muscle cars. Explore our rich heritage through the decades.

The Birth of an Icon

Introduced at the World's Fair in 1964, the original Mustang created the "pony car" class and became an instant sensation.

1964

Fastback Design

The Mustang Fastback was launched, featuring a sleek design and improved aerodynamics that would become legendary.

1965

GT Fastback

The Mustang GT Fastback became famous in the movie "Bullitt," starring Steve McQueen in one of cinema's greatest chase scenes.

1968

Mach 1 Debut

The high-performance Mach 1 package was introduced, becoming an instant classic with its bold styling and powerful engines.

1969

Mustang II

A smaller, more fuel-efficient Mustang debuted in response to the oil crisis, keeping the brand alive during challenging times.

1974
